About Us

Willify is a renowned music streaming platform that made its debut in 2006. Originating as a digital audio service, Willify quickly gained prominence and has become a household name in the world of music. Starting with a vision to revolutionize how people consume music, Willify has grown into a global phenomenon with millions of active users.

"Willify: Revolutionizing Music, One Track at a Time."

Our History

Founded in 2006, Willify started as a small digital audio service with the goal of making all the world's music instantly available. It has since grown into a global music streaming leader, boasting millions of tracks and expanding into podcasts. Key milestones include launching a mobile app in 2008, introducing personalized playlists in 2012, reaching 100 million users in 2016, and adding podcasts in 2020.
